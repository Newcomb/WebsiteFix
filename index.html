Design

When you use our CSS to style the webpage you will be enveloped with a medieval theme. It should feel like you are sitting in an ancient library, reading off of parchment paper, leather, and wood. We picked two fonts to help support our Theme, one being a medieval styled one and the other being more flowy to represent the emphasis on caligraphy during that time period. The setup is simplistic to represent how things would have been hundreds of years ago.

New Features

In addition to the theme, we changed the navigation center so it is vertically aligned and increased the font size for accessibility. We also made it fixed for easy access while scrolling through the site. Along with the changes to the navigaiton, we also realigned the partner information so that it is vertical. The class material is now much easier to read and access due to margins and/or padding on most of the elements. In the prep and lab links the prompt, instructions, and scoring sections are all seperated by different backgrounds. Classmates are placed onto a leather strip and seating is on a horizontal scroll similar to a map. The account page features a vertical parchment scroll that the user writes their information on.
